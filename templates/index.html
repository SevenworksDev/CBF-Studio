Ever wanted to build a simple Geometry Dash bot for free without decent knowledge on coding?
You're in luck!

How does it work?

Using the Dashboard, you can manage all your Geometry Dash bots. To create a new bot, you can click the "Create Bot" button which pops up a form.
After submitting, you are brought to the Bot Editor where you can edit commands and other information.
Submitting "Bot Account" details is safe and secure and will not be shared to anyone. (these details wont be validated by RobTops servers)
DON'T BE STUPID! Your bot account needs to be a fresh account, GDFactory is 100% bannable by Geometry Dash moderation (for some reason)
